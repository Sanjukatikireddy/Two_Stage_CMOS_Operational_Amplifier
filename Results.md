## Power Consumption

P = (V<sub>DD</sub> - V<sub>SS</sub>) x I<sub>Supply</sub>

I<sub>Supply</sub> = 0.13 mA

V<sub>DD</sub> = 2.5 V and V<sub>SS</sub> = -2.5 V

P = 5 x 0.13 mW

P = 0.65 mW

The average power consumed is 0.65 mW.
